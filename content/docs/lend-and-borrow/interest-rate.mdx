## Borrow Interest Rate

The borrow interest rate for the DeepDEX lending protocol is determined by a multi-linear model. This model features a tiered structure with a defined minimum rate, three kink points, and a maximum rate at 100% utilization. For each asset, the following parameters are defined:

- Minimum rate $$R_\text{min}$$
- Utilization rates ($$U_1$$, $$U_2$$, $$U_3$$, etc.) and the corresponding interest rates ($$R_1$$, $$R_2$$, $$R_3$$, etc.) at multiple (up to five) kinks
- Maximum rate $$R_\text{max}$$ at 100% utilization

## Supply Interest Rate

The supply interest rate is derived from the borrow rate, utilization ($$U$$), and the [reserve factor](/glossary#reserve-factor) ($$\rho$$):

```math
R_\text{supply} = R_\text{borrow} \times U \times (1 - \rho)
```
